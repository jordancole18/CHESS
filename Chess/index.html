What is CHESS?

The Cluster Habitat for Educational Server Solution. CHESS is a prepacked method to connect idle hardware into a general purpose cluster web server and supercomputer. Full mission statement here.

Hosting Needs

Educational computer labs have more than enough idle resources for CHESS to use for all hosting needs, from student blog to school main site and more.

Intelligent Balancing

Our proprietary load balancer intelligently manages requests based on individual node resources flexibly even during student use, reducing performance issues.

Harnessing Power

The average hoghschool computer lab has in excess of 10 TeraFLOPS of untapped computing power, CHESS unlocks this huge potential.

Cost Reduction

Locally hosting eliminates the need for pricey services or dedicated hardware, saving schools, clubs, and students money, removing barriers to reaping the potential of online presence.

STEM Programs

The creative and configurable nature of CHESS makes it a tool to teach previously inaccessable topics, everything from full stack development to scientific computing.

Plug and Play

CHESS is easily installed on future nodes, and a user friendly setup wizard quickly connects nodes into a cluster, requiring minimal computer savy.
